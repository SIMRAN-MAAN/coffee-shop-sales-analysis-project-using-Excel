# coffee-shop-sales-analysis-project-using-Excel
Coffee Shop Sales Analysis (Excel)

**Project Overview**

This project analyzes coffee shop sales data using Microsoft Excel to uncover key business insights and sales trends. The analysis focuses on understanding customer purchasing behavior, product performance, peak sales periods, and overall revenue generation.

**Objectives**
- Analyze daily, weekly, and monthly sales performance.
- Identify top-selling products and categories.
- Examine sales trends across different time periods.
- Evaluate customer purchasing patterns.
- Generate actionable insights to support business decision-making.

**Tools Used**

- Microsoft Excel
  - Pivot Tables
  - Pivot Charts
  - Slicers
  - Conditional Formatting
  - Data Cleaning & Transformation
  - Dashboard Creation

**Key Insights**

- Identified peak sales hours and busiest days.
- Determined the highest revenue-generating products.
- Analyzed category-wise sales contribution.
- Evaluated monthly sales trends and growth patterns.
- Created an interactive dashboard for data visualization and reporting.

**Dashboard Features**

- Total Sales Overview
- Sales by Product Category
- Revenue Trends
- Sales by Day and Hour
- Interactive Filters and Slicers
- KPI Metrics for Business Performance
